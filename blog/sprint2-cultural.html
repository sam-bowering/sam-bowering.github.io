14/07/2019

What is your long term goal and or career pathway?

Robotics, artificial intelligence or private security sector. There’s nothing I’d love to do more than get front-line soldiers off the streets and have hunks of metal with a couple computers in them risk their shiny polishes than fathers, mothers, brothers and sisters lives.

What skills (non-technical - human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at EDA?

Interview skills and working with people I don’t share the same views with. Whenever I’m talking to someone with strong opposing views to those I hold myself, I quickly find myself feeling this disgusting sense of hatred and my brain instantly switches to not hearing anything the person says whether it makes sense or not and immediately dismisses it.

A description of your own strengths and limitations

Strengths and Weakness Blog - Here I talk about my strengths and weakness being overthinking, find it difficult to focus, highly emotional but also curious, know how to relax and one I left out, appreciation of beauty. I personally believe my weakness are completely controllable and only show their face when my strengths don’t quite pull through. I like to think of myself as someone who can put their all into a task but also hold the ability to step away from it and appreciate what’s been achieved by myself and or others. When I mentioned I believed my emotions are controllable, I meant that in the sense that if I sat and thought on what I was doing/about to do, I would understand whatever that was is wrong and consciously choose not to do it. But no ones perfect and there isn’t enough time in the world to stop and second guess everything you do.

Anywhere I can get it from? If you need a question answered… Ask. Doing so in a timely way would simply be asking someone face to face that I think will be able to answer my question.
